# 🐝 Spelling Bee

**Play it here: <https://thomassymann.github.io/kids-spelling/>**

A friendly spelling practice app for kids from kindergarten to 4th grade. The app reads an English word out loud (and uses it in a short sentence) — your child types what they hear. Correct answers earn stars and streaks; after three tries the app shows the right spelling and moves on. No accounts, no ads, no internet needed after the first visit.

## How to play

1. **Pick a grade** at the top: **K**, **1**, **2**, **3**, or **4**. The word lists grow with each grade — from sight words like *cat* and *play* up to 4th-grade words like *neighbor* and *remember*. (The 🧪 tab is a small demo set.)
2. **Listen.** Each new word is spoken aloud, followed by an example sentence. The dotted slots show how many letters the word has.
3. **Type the word** on the letter keyboard and press the green ✔.
4. **Need help?**
   - 🔊 repeats the word
   - 💬 repeats the example sentence
   - 👁 flashes the word for a moment (look, cover, write!)

Got it right? 🎉 On to the next word. Got it wrong? Two more tries, then the app shows the correct spelling before moving on.

The top bar keeps score: ✔ correct, ✘ wrong, and 🔥 your current streak.

## Add it to your phone or tablet (PWA)

Spelling Bee is a Progressive Web App: installed once, it works like a normal app — full screen, home-screen icon, and fully offline (words already practiced are stored on the device).

### iPhone / iPad (Safari)

1. Open <https://thomassymann.github.io/kids-spelling/> in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow, at the bottom on iPhone / top right on iPad).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**. The 🐝 icon appears on your home screen — launch it from there.

> Tip: open the app once while online and tap through a few words in each grade you plan to use, so their audio is saved for offline use.

### Android (Chrome)

1. Open <https://thomassymann.github.io/kids-spelling/> in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **⋮ menu** in the top-right corner.
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** (on some devices: **Install app**).
4. Confirm with **Install** / **Add**.

The app opens full screen from its home-screen icon, no browser bars.

## Sound

- The words are pre-recorded with a clear, friendly voice — best experienced with the volume up.
- iPhones/iPads: if you hear nothing, check that the mute switch isn't on; the first word may need a tap on 🔊 (browsers only allow sound after a touch).
